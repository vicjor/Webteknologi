Our service

Mediafy is a service created by co-founders William Østensen, Victor Jørgensen and Erling Olweus. All three authors are students at NTNU in the course IT2805 Webteknologi. The idea behind Mediafy is to let the user explore new music, movies and TV shows.

Purpose and goals

Mediafy's purpose is to deliver top recommendations of the best music, movies and TV-shows to media enthusiats. The goal of Mediafy is to recommend trending music, media and TV, editors picks and IMDB recommendations while giving people a easy-to-use and clean site to learn about the newest within music, movies and series.

1. The front page - index.html

The front page, index.html, will be the first page to appear when visiting the website. The front page will link to several subpages, but not all of them. On the top of the page there will be clickable images to editors pick, this weeks artist, series to watch this fall and this week in musiv. A javascript application will make sure that different articles will be shown. In the center we will have a slider through all three categories of the page; music, film and series. We are planning on using a javascript application for this slider. At the bottom of the front page, and also at the bottom of every subpage, we will have a footer with a subscription button to our newsletter, and generel information a pseudo links to forum, jobs, help, etc. To avoid repetition will will not type this in in all of the following page descriptions.
