Functional and non-functional requirements

  • Dark design, with clean look and vintage vibes
  • Subscription to news letter
  • A slider with clickable images to this weeks picks, movies, music, artists etc.
  • In artist pages, link to Spotify and social media
  • The menu bar should be fixed, so that it is always visible to the user, regardless of if the user has scrolled down.
  • One subpage each for music, TV and movies.
  • IMDB rating on movies
  • The user should be able to get to the music, movie and TV subpage in two clicks or less.
