18.09.2018

Our service

Mediafy is a service created by co-founders William Østensen, Victor Jørgensen and Erling Olweus. All three authors are students at NTNU in the course IT2805 Webteknologi. The idea behind Mediafy is to let the user explore new music, movies and TV shows.

Purpose and goals

This site purpose and goal is to deliver top recommendations of the best music, movies and TV-shows to media enthusiats. Mediafy will recommend trending music, media and TV, editors picks and reviews while giving people an easy-to-use and clean site to learn about the latest within music, movies and series.

Audience

Mediafy’s target audience will mainly be people who wants recommendations for music to check out, movies and TV-shows to watch and generally wants to expand their knowledge of whats new and cool within these topics. The typical visitor to Mediafy is more interested in these topics than the average person, and wishes to find other suggestions than the top 50 spotify music, box office breaking movies and streaming services recommendations.

Content of Mediafy and how it is organized

FRONT PAGE

The front page will have a slider-view with the different «This weeks top picks» and will also show the latest articles posted. It will also display a menu-bar with links to the different sub-pages and have information about the page and what it delivers

ABOUT

This page will contain more in-depth information about the page, what the page delivers and contact information.

MUSIC, MOVIES AND SERIES

Each of the topics will have a own sub-page containing articles and recommendations. From this page you can click on individual articles about the latest in the chosen topic and also see «Editors Picks» and «This weeks ...»

SUBSCRIBE

Mediafy will also have a subscribe-page where you can write down your email and subscribe to Mediafy's newsletter and get notified whenever fresh content is uploaded to the page and to keep yourself updated in music, movies and series

Functional requirements

  • The page will have subscription to news letter
  • The index page will have a slider with clickable images(links) to this weeks picks, movies, music, artists etc.
  • Mentioned artists/bands in different articles will have links to their music(Spotify) and social media
  • The menu bar should be fixed, so that it is always visible to the user, regardless if the user has scrolled down.

Non-functional requirements

  • Mediafy will have a light design, and is going for a vintage vibe
  • The website will be user friendly and easy to navigate
  • The user should be able to get to the music, movie and TV subpage in two clicks or less.
  • One subpage each for music, TV and movies.
