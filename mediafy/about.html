About Mediafy

Mediafy is a serice created by co-founders William Østensen, Victor Jørgensen and Erling Olweus. All three founders are students in computers science at NTNU, and is taking the course IT2805 Webtechnology. The idea behind Mediafy is to let the users explore new music, movies and TV shows.



Mediafy's purpose is to deliver top recommendations of the best music, movies and TV-shows to media enthusiasts. The goal of Mediafy is to recommend trending music, media and TV, editors picks and IMDB recommendations while giving people a easy-to-use and clean site to learn about the newest within usiv, movies and series.
