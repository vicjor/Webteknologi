This Weeks Artist: Sam Fender

Sam Fender is an aspiring up and coming musician aiming for greatness, and is completely nailing it. With his raw guitar anthems and alternative rock sound every song he has released has been a hit, without exceptions! Sam is not just your average musician writing love clichés, just the opposite actually. Instead Sam writes about important political and social issues like fake news, government surveillance, sexual harrasment and mental health and with his newly released single «Dead boys» he tackles “toxic masculinity” and mental health issues in his home town.

Also named one of BBC’s sound of the year alongside other emerging artist like Sigrid and Khalid, Sam Fender has a great carreer in the music industry coming and we cant wait to hear more from him.
