11/01/2018 By William Østensen

Editor's Picks:

Great music is everywhere and in all types of genres, however its not always easy to discover new music and you can easily be stuck to one genre and listening to the same music over and over again. The Mediafy music editors therefore got together and voted on our all time favorites and hope that sharing these can help you discover some great new music.

The Neighbourhood

The Neighbourhood (aka "THE NBHD") is an American rock band formed in Newbury Park, California, in 2011. The band is composed of vocalist Jesse Rutherford, guitarists Jeremy Freedman and Zach Abels, bassist Mikey Margott, and drummer Brandon Alexander Fried. With EP´s Hard and To Imagine and their self titled album together with their most recently EP Ever Changing in the newly released album projectHard To Imagine The Neighbourhood Ever Changing they've kept busy this year. With music featuring raw vocals mixed with down-tempo rock and R&B-sound, The Neighbourhood scores high on the list and is definitely worth checking out.

Hobo Johnson

Frank Lopes Jr., known professionally as Hobo Johnson, is an American vocalist and frontman of Hobo Johnson and the LoveMakers. Him and his bands appearance in NPR's Tiny Desk Contest with their song Peach Scone collected them millions of views and also earned them a own Tiny Desk Concert. Johnson and the LoveMakers gives you tracks filled with emotion and almost a talkative vibe to the vocals(not singing, not rap). We will most certainly keep our heads up for these guys.

Follow Hobo Johnson On Social Media:

Anderson.Paak

Brandon Paak Anderson, better known by his stage name Anderson.Paak, is an American musician and record producer from Oxnard, California. With his 2016 released album Malibu ,which earned him a nomination for «Best Urban Contemporary Album» at the Grammy award, is filled with sick drumbeats and raspy vocals from Anderson himself and raw musical tracks from the band. His newest release Oxnard is coming out on November 16th and we cant wait to hear it and so should you.

Sam Fender

Sam Fender, also elected Mediafy's Artist Of The Week, is an aspiring up and coming musician aiming for greatness, and is completely nailing it. Writing about important political, social and mental health issues, Fender really brings out a serious message in his music. Him and his newest release coming out November 20th is definitely not something you wanna miss out on. (For more information check out This Weeks Artist.)

Follow Sam Fender On Social Media:

Jordan Rakei

Jordan Rakei (born 23 May 1992) is a New Zealander-Australian musician, vocalist and producer currently based in London, UK. Rakei has also released and performed under the moniker Dan Kye. Rakei has two albums Cloak(2016) and Wallflower(2017) and his music is a really good listen. If you're looking for music with emotionally charged vocals and impeccable disclosure with jazzy and bluesy melodies, Jordan is most certainly your guy.

Follow Jordan Rakei On Social Media:

Nothing But Thieves

Nothing But Thieves are an English alternative rock band from Southend-on-Sea, Essex. They consist of lead vocalist and guitarist Conor Mason, guitarist Joe Langridge-Brown, guitarist and keyboardist Dominic Craik, bassist Philip Blake, and drummer James Price. Their style of music featuring insane vocals from Mason over undisguised rock tracks has been compared to the likes of Foals, The Neighbourhood, and Civil Twilight, and they have been played on alternative and active rock radio all around the world.

Emilie Nicolas

Emilie Nicolas Kongshavn is a Norwegian singer-songwriter. Her songs are filled with personal lyrics, rythmic tracks and an insanely controlled voice. Her newest album release Tranquille Emile really makes those goosebumps go all the way to your neck.

Tom Misch

English musician and producer Tom Misch began releasing music on SoundCloud in 2012 and just this year released his debut studio album Geography featuring rappers like GoldLink, De La Soul and Loyle Carner. His music is packed groovy beats, smooth and clean vocals with jazzy guitar tracks that really gives you that groovy grimace.

Follow Tom Misch On Social Media:

Dermot Kennedy

Life and death. Joy and pain. Love and loss. Dermot Kennedy has always been fascinated by extremes. On his brilliant debut EP, 'Doves & Ravens,' the young Dubliner draws inspiration from all the moments of brightness and darkness this world has to offer, crafting music that's at once soaring and intimate, stripped-back and explosive. With his emotional and rough voice his music and message unquestiably something every man with respect for music has to check out. (Visit dermotkennedy.com for more info).

Follow Dermot Kennedy On Social Media:

Loyle Carner

Benjamin Gerard Coyle-Larner, better known by the stage name Loyle Carner, is an English hip hop musician. His sound has been described by NME as "sensitive and eloquent" and by The Guardian as "confessional hip-hop". Carner's debut album, Yesterday's Gone, was nominated for the 2017 Mercury Prize and Carner has both in former and later releases worked with Tom Misch and Jordan Rakei.
