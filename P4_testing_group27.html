Response

It is impossible to please everyone and that goes for this as well. We got some good feedback on the design and layout with testers saying that they liked it and some not so good from testers who didnt like it. This we totally understand. Some of the points commented on was the colors choices for the page.

As for the colors we mainly used a color/palette tool to choose different matching colors, however some of the colors were improvised and this can quickly make the site look a bit «cheap» or amateuer and not so thought through. This is something we definitely would look more into and spend some more time on and change. Color choices are a big part of the design and therefore if we were to change something to the page it would be to be more careful with the colordesign. Unfortunately because of some major functional issues on the page that took up most of the time, color choices and design were a bit down-prioritized.

Another thing that could be improved is the images used. we could edit the pictures and frontpageimages better such that no images end up stretched or placing another image as a background. All in all make the images fit bettrer into the page.

Some of the feedback also adressed that some of the elements of the page were a little «flashy» and coukd come across as disturbing. These could be made a bit more subtle and incorparated better into the page.
